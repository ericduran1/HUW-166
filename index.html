What is my Favorite movie?

My favorite movie is probably "The Hateful Eight".
This movie was directed by one of my favorite movie
directors Quentin Tarantino. The budget for the movie
was 44-55 million dollars for a film that is 3 hours and 7
minutes long.

Why is it my Favorite Movie?

It's really my favorite movie because the story is so well written.
Also one of my favorite actor's "Samuel L. Jackson" is in the film.
I like that Quentin Tarantino works closely with Samuel L. Jackson in so many of
his films because he's a good actor. All i want to say about this film is that
if you've seen any of Tarantino's films it's exactly what you expect, this film has alot
of dialogue but the violence scenes are amazing.

Why you should see it?

If you love films that have good revenge plots you'll love this film.
This film is up there with my favorite film's "Kill Bill Volume 1", 2 and
"Pulp Fiction". P.S if you're uncomfortable with partial nudity
and blood you probably wont like this film.
